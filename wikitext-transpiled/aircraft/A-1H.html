Description[edit | edit source]

The A-1H Skyraider is a premium gift rank IV American strike aircraft with a battle rating of 6.3 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Fire and Ice" as a reward for Battle Pass: Season IX, "Smell of Victory".
