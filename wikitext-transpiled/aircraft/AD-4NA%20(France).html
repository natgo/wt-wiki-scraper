Description[edit | edit source]

The ▄AD-4NA Skyraider is a premium rank IV French strike aircraft with a battle rating of 5.7 (AB) and 6.0 (RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Ground Breaking".

Offensive armament[edit | edit source]

Main article: M3 (20 mm)

The AD-4NA (France) is armed with:

  • 4 x 20 mm M3 cannons, wing-mounted (200 rpg = 800 total)
