Description[edit | edit source]

The Aichi E13A1 Model 11 is a gift rank I Japanese bomber with a battle rating of 1.3 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Winged Lions" as a ship-launched reconnaissance aircraft.

Ships that carry the E13A1

  • IJN Agano
  • IJN Mogami
  • IJN Kongo
  • IJN Haruna
