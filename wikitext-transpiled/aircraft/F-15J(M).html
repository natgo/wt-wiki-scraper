Description[edit | edit source]

The F-15J(M) Eagle is a rank VIII Japanese jet fighter with a battle rating of 13.7 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Seek & Destroy".

Survivability and armour[edit | edit source]

The F-15 was designed with a focus on survivability. It can often survive non-direct missile hits and still be operational enough to land, compared to planes like the Mirage 2000 or F-16. The F-15 has no armour protection, but the high capacity of countermeasures (240) helps with decoying incoming missiles.
