Pros and cons[edit | edit source]

Pros:

  • 15 mm cannon does decent damage for its BR
  • Is quite durable, can take many MG rounds to take down
  • Can fly back on 1 engine, although careful management of WEP is required

Cons:

  • Poor speed and manoeuvrability, many fighters at its BR can catch up to if it descends from bomber altitude
  • Poor defensive protection
  • Major blindspots in defensive coverage, especially below the aircraft
  • Poor dive speed, can rip its wing easily
