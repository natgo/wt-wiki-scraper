Description[edit | edit source]

The Loire 130С is a gift rank I French bomber with a battle rating of 1.3 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Sons of Attila" as a ship-launched reconnaissance aircraft.

Ships that carry the Loire 130C:

  • Colbert
  • Dupleix
