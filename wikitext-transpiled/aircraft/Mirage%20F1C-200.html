Description[edit | edit source]

The Mirage F1C-200 is a premium gift rank VII French jet fighter with a battle rating of 11.0 (AB/SB) and 11.3 (RB). It was introduced in Update "Apex Predators".

Offensive armament[edit | edit source]

Main article: DEFA 553 (30 mm)

The Mirage F1C-200 is armed with:

  • A choice between two presets:
    • 2 x 30 mm DEFA 553 cannons, belly-mounted (135 rpg = 270 total)
    • 2 x 30 mm DEFA 553 cannons + 252 x countermeasures
