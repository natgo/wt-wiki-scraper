Description[edit | edit source]

The OS3U-1 Corsair is a gift rank I American bomber with a battle rating of 1.3 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Dance of Dragons" as a ship-launched reconnaissance aircraft.

Ships that carry the O3U-1:

  • USS Arizona
  • USS Trenton
