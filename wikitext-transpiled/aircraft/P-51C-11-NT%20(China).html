Description[edit | edit source]

The ␗P-51C-11-NT is a premium gift rank III Chinese fighter with a battle rating of 3.7 (AB/RB) and 4.0 (SB). It was introduced during Update "Alpha Strike" as a reward for Battle Pass: Season XV, "Northern King".

Offensive armament[edit | edit source]

Main article: M2 Browning (12.7 mm)

The P-51C-11-NT (China) is armed with:

  • 4 x 12.7 mm M2 Browning machine guns, wing-mounted (280 rpg outer + 350 rpg inner = 1,260 total)

Suspended armament[edit | edit source]

Default weapon presets
  • Without load
  • 2 x 100 lb AN-M30A1 bombs (200 lb total)
  • 2 x 250 lb AN-M57 bombs (500 lb total)
  • 2 x 500 lb AN-M64A1 bombs (1,000 lb total)
