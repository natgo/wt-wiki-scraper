Description[edit | edit source]

The Walrus Mk.I is a gift rank I British bomber with a battle rating of 1.3 (AB/SB) and 1.0 (RB). It was introduced in Update "Winged Lions" as a ship-launched reconnaissance aircraft.

Ships that carry the Walrus

  • HMS York
  • HMS Southampton
