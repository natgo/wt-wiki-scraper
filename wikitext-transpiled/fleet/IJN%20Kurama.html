Description[edit | edit source]

The Kurama-class, IJN Kurama, 1920 is a gift rank V Japanese battlecruiser with a battle rating of 6.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced during Update "Sons of Attila" as a reward for the 2023 Tokushu Heiki event.
