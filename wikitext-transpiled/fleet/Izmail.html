Description[edit | edit source]

The Izmail-class, Izmail, project is a rank VI Soviet battlecruiser with a battle rating of 7.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Dance of Dragons".

Pros and cons[edit | edit source]

Pros:

  • Extremely potent HE shells
  • Powerful and versatile main armament
  • Well armoured turrets
  • Numerous and effective secondaries

Cons:

  • Lack of usable anti-air armament
  • Highly vulnerable crew compartments
  • Prone to ammunition explosions
  • Turning performance is subpar
  • Ineffective deck and belt armour
