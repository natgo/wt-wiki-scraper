Description[edit | edit source]

The S 26-class, MC 485 is a premium gift rank III Italian motor torpedo boat with a battle rating of 2.7 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "La Royale" as a reward for Battle Pass: Season XII, "Armour Breaking Ambusher".
