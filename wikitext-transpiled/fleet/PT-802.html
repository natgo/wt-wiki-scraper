Description[edit | edit source]

The Type 1, PT-802 is a rank II Japanese motor torpedo boat with a battle rating of 2.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Dance of Dragons".

Pros and cons[edit | edit source]

Pros:

  • The Bofors L/60 Mark 3 autocannon provides significant firepower to destroy enemy torpedo boats at similar BR.
  • 18 members is a slightly above average crew count for torpedo boats, which leads to increased survivability.
  • The PT-802 has access to 7.2 in T37 rocket-powered depth charges.

Cons:

  • Very uncomfortable gun placement: Superstructure and torpedo tubes make it impossible to shoot forward or sideways. Only very limited firing angles are accessible.
  • Unsatisfactory maximum speed for a torpedo boat: It can barely keep up with early destroyers. Torpedo assaults require antecedent positioning behind cover.
  • Wooden structure is not able to stand against even low caliber machine guns.
  • No secondary or AA armament: Incoming enemy aircraft must be noticed in time and shot down manually.
  • Type 54 model 3 torpedoes have low explosive mass and belong to the slowest torpedoes in the game.
