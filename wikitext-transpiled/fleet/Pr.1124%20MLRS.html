Description[edit | edit source]

The Pr.1124 MLRS is a premium gift rank II Soviet armoured gun boat with a battle rating of 2.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was one of the earliest pre-orders offered to access the fleet closed beta test before being removed from the store with the wide-release of the closed beta test in Update 1.79 "Project X", though it was not available to be used until the Soviet fleet was introduced during 1.79.
