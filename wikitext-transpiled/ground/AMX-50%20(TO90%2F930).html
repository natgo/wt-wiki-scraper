Description[edit | edit source]

The AMX Char Moyen de 50 tonnes (TO90/930) is a premium gift rank IV French medium tank with a battle rating of 7.3 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Apex Predators" as a reward for Battle Pass: Season X, "Royal Guard".
