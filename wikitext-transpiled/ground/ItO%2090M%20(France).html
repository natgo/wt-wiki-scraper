Description[edit | edit source]

The ▄Ilmatorjuntaohjus 90M is a rank VII French SPAA with a battle rating of 11.0 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Red Skies".

Pros and cons[edit | edit source]

Pros:

  • One of the longest ranges in the game at a maximum range of 12 km
  • VT1 missiles come stock and they are very fast
  • Has access to gen 1 gunner thermals
  • Can fire all 8 missiles without reloading

Cons:

  • 8 mm of all-around armour is insufficient to stop even .50 cal machine guns
  • Search radar when unfolded sticks out quite far giving it a very tall profile
  • Lacks any sort of self-defence against ground targets
  • Lacks any sort of mobility offroad or uphill
