Description[edit | edit source]

The Leopard 2A7HU is a rank VIII Hungarian medium tank with a battle rating of 11.7 (AB/RB/SB) in the Italian research tree. It was introduced in Update "Seek & Destroy" as part of the Hungarian branch of the Italian tech tree.

Pros and cons[edit | edit source]

Pros:

  • Marvelous penetration with the DM53 round
  • Upgraded armour making the tank hard to kill from the front
  • Excellent reverse and forward speed
  • Has access to Gen 3 thermals for the commander and gunner unlike previous Leopards
  • Perfect gun handling for an MBT: 40°/s for both turret rotation and gun elevation speed (with ace crew) and -9°/+20° maximum vertical guidance
  • Access to a top mounted .50 cal machine gun
  • Access to HE-TF round that can deal with lightly armoured targets
  • Access to spall liners that increase survivability
  • Quite survivable tank with a lot of space between each modules and crew members

Cons:

  • The turret armour can be shot off making the tank more vulnerable
  • Turret ring and mantlet are still a huge weak spot, despite the added armour
  • 12.7 turret is quite large. Makes the tank easier to spot and kill with large caliber HE
  • Engine deck prevents from lowering the gun enough towards the back
  • Mine protection adds a lot of weight and decrease the mobility compared to a standard Leopard 2A7V
