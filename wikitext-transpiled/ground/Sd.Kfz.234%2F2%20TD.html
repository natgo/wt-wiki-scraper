Description[edit | edit source]

The s.Pz.Sp.Wg. (5 cm 39/1 L60) is a gift rank II German light tank with a battle rating of 2.7 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced during Update "Ixwa Strike" as a reward for watching Enlisted streams on Twitch.tv.
