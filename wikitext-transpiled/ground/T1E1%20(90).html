Description[edit | edit source]

Notes:

  • Ammo rack 7 is a ready rack, and takes priority in being filled at the beginning of the battle.
  • Full reload speed will be realized as long as ammo exists in the ready rack. If the ready rack is empty, a penalty to reload speed will occur.
  • Not firing when the gun is loaded will load ammo from racks 1-6 into rack 7, as long as there is ammo in racks 1-6. Firing will interrupt the loading of ready racks.
