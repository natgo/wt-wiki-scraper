Description[edit | edit source]

The HKP3C is a rank V Swedish utility helicopter with a battle rating of 7.7 (AB/RB/SB). It was introduced in Update "Wind of Change".

Pros and cons[edit | edit source]

Pros:

  • Good top speed
  • Fairly agile
  • Equipped with 6 very manoeuvrable anti-tank missiles

Cons:

  • Slow missiles with a very short range
  • No guns or rockets
  • No night vision devices
  • No way to shoot down enemy aircraft
